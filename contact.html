Contact

  • If you are interested in joining our team, please send an email to Dr. Lee. Please include your CV and a brief description about how your research interests fit to our lab in your email. Our lab welcomes enthusiastic applicants from diverse backgrounds including (but not limited to) architectural engineering, acoustics, psychology, statistics, mathematics, music and sound.

  • In summer semesters, research intern positions are (usually) also open to undergraduate students from Concordia and Other Universities.
